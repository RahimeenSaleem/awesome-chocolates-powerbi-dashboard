# 🍫 Awesome Chocolates Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze the sales performance of Awesome Chocolates across multiple countries, product categories, and sales teams.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor sales trends, evaluate salesperson performance, compare regional sales, and forecast future business performance through interactive visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Sales managers need a centralized dashboard to answer questions such as:

- Which countries generate the highest sales?
- Which product teams perform best?
- Which salespeople contribute the most revenue?
- Which product categories are the most profitable?
- What are the expected future sales trends?

This dashboard provides quick answers to these questions using interactive Power BI reports.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Geographical Sales Analysis

This page provides an overview of sales performance across all countries where Awesome Chocolates operates.

### Features

- Sales by Country
- Sales by Product Team
- Salesperson Filter
- Interactive Cross Filtering

### Business Insights

- Compare sales performance between countries.
- Identify the strongest-performing product teams.
- Filter reports by individual salesperson.

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Performance Report

This page focuses on salesperson and product performance.

### Features

- Salesperson Performance Table
- Employee Images
- Total Sales Amount
- Total Boxes Sold
- Average Revenue per Box
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Country
- Team Slicer

### Business Insights

- Identify top-performing sales representatives.
- Compare product category revenue.
- Analyze regional sales performance.
- Evaluate sales efficiency.

---

## 3️⃣ Sales Trend & Forecasts

This page analyzes historical sales performance and predicts future trends using Power BI forecasting.

### Features

- Customer Trend Analysis
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Built-in Forecasting
- Confidence Interval Visualization

### Business Insights

- Monitor customer growth over time.
- Forecast future sales.
- Support inventory and business planning.
